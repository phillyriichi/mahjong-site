We are happy to be holding the first Riichi Open tournament in the greater Philadelphia area!

With walking distance to great areas like Reading Terminal Market, Chinatown, and City Hall, the Convention Center provides a great central location for players from within Philadelphia and those visiting us from elsewhere.

Hope to see you in Philadelphia!

General Info

Tournament Dates: March 23 - 24 (Saturday and Sunday)
(Free play will be available on Friday)

Location: Pennsylvania Convention Center Room 125 (1101 Arch St, Philadelphia, PA 19107 - Enter from the corner of Arch St and N Broad St)

Entry Fee: $65

Player Cap: 80 players

Tournament Discord: https://discord.gg/wEufVSPJs7

Food: Due to the large number of food options nearby, food will not be provided at the venue. Extra time will be allotted during the lunch breaks to accommodate this. Water and coffee will be provided.

Registration

Registration Begins: December 15 (Friday) at 4:00 PM EST (1:00 PM PST)

Payment Deadline to Confirm Registration: January 31 (Wednesday)
After this point, any players on the waitlist will have an opportunity to confirm a spot.

Tournament Info

Ruleset: WRC standard ruleset with Red Dora (Aka-dora) modifer (https://worldriichi.org/wrc-rules)

This tournament has been approved as a WRC Qualifier. For details, see https://www.nariichi.org/wrc-2025-qualification

Timers: Games will be 75 minutes + 1 hand. When time is called, any tables not completed will finish their current hand, and play 1 additional hand.

Format: All players will play 8 hanchan with cumulative score. At the end of the 8th hanchan, placement will be determined by the final score rankings. There are no score cuts or resets.

Prizes: Top 4 players receive trophies. 5th through 8th receive medals.

Schedule: Free Play will also be available Friday, March 22 from 2 PM - 11 PM
