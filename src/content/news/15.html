Philly Riichi Open 2024 Wrap-up

PreviousNext

Philly Riichi Open 2024 is over, and was a great success!

It was so great seeing a lot of friends, old and new, come by and take part in our first open Mahjong tournament. Big congrats to Edward K. for taking 1st place, and congratulations to the top 16 for winning points towards the WRC Qualifying tournament.

We look forward to see everyone again in a future event, either back in Philly, or in the next city. Until next time!
