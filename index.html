Welcome to Philadelphia Riichi Mah-Jawn

About Us

We are a Japanese (Riichi) Mahjong club based out of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. We have weekly club meetings, as well as frequent meetups outside of the club.

Many of our members also often travel across the country, visiting our friends at other clubs, or attending conventions and tournaments.

If you are new to Mahjong and interested in learning, or have played Riichi (or any other type of Mahjong!) before and want to learn more, or you're a veteran interested in playing with other seasoned players, come check us out!

Join Us

Our current club meetings are every Saturday from 11:30am until 3 pm at Thirsty Dice in Fairmount Avenue (https://www.thirstydice.com/).

We also meet every Wednesday from 6:30pm until 10pm at the King of Prussia Bridge Club. (Check out our Meetup for more info: https://www.meetup.com/phillymahjong/)
